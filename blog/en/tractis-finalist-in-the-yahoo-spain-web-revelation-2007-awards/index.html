Tractis, finalist in the Yahoo! Spain Web Revelation 2007 Awards

Following our launch in Spain last October, I’m pleased to report that Tractis has been selected as a finalist for the Yahoo! Spain Web Revelation 2007 Awards. Tractis has been included in the “Finance & Employment” category, which seems an “elastic” category for generic business/commerce apps.

I’m not going to be so desperately mercantilistic as to suggest that you run to vote for us, but I will say this: Imagine the joy on the faces of your loved ones when they find that Tractis has won the 17th of January .

By David Blanco
