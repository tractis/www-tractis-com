Tractis Reader Packs: get readers free with every purchase

At the end of 2011 we created “Tractis Hardware”, a new family of Tractis services aimed at promoting the massive deployment of smart card readers in all the countries where we operate.

We began with the opening of the Tractis Store, our on-line store. Next came the offering of readers at heavily discounted prices to political initiatives and start-ups. Today we take things one step forward by facilitating the acquisition of readers at low cost.

Discover the new Tractis “Reader Packs”

As from today your business or association can acquire “reader packs” on the Tractis Store and obtain free readers with every purchase.

We have started with two reader packs: the 10+1 Pack and the 100+20 Pack. Obviously, you can keep on buying readers one by one at a price of €19,99, VAT and express courier delivery included.

10+1 Pack

With every purchase of a 10 Tractis Slim Readers Pack (€199,90) you get 1 free reader.

This represents a unit cost of €18,17 per reader, or 1 free reader for every 10 bought.

100+20 Pack

With every purchase of a 100 Tractis Slim Readers Pack (€1999,00) you get 20 free readers.

This represents a unit cost of €16,65 per reader, or 1 free reader for every 5 bought.

As with every other item in the Tractis store, the new “Reader Packs” are compatible with all operating systems, their prices include VAT and delivery and are sent by express courier within 24 hours.
